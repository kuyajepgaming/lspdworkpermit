Executive Directive for San Andreas Mining Safety Regulations

This Work Permit authorizes the individual identified above to engage in lawful mining operations within designated mining locations throughout the State of San Andreas.

This permit remains valid only while the holder complies with all provisions established under the Executive Directive for San Andreas Mining Safety Regulations.

Conditions of this Permit

  • Wear a Hard Hat at all times.
  • Wear a Reflective Safety Vest.
  • Wear Work Boots suitable for mining operations.
  • Carry this Work Permit while conducting mining activities.
  • Present this permit immediately upon request by any Law Enforcement Officer or authorized Government Official.
Failure to comply with the above requirements may result in immediate revocation of this permit, citations, fines, or additional enforcement action under the Executive Directive for San Andreas Mining Safety Regulations.
